**Q: How is CIx different from traditional CI tools?**

Traditional CI tools require manual updates and exist as separate platforms - CIx meets your teams where they work.

**Q: Do I need a dedicated CI team to use CIx?**
A: No. CIx is designed for both dedicated CI teams and companies without CI resources. Solo founders, lean startups, and individual contributors can use CIx effectively. For enterprises with CI teams, CIx amplifies their impact by automating routine work.

**Q: Can I customize CIx for my company?**

**Q: How long does implementation take?**
A: Most teams are up and running the same day. Add competitors, generate battlecards, and start enabling teams within hours. Enterprise implementations with custom integrations typically take 1-2 weeks.

**Q: What integrations does CIx support?**
