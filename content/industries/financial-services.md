## Overview (350 words)

**H2:** Financial Services Competitive Dynamics

Financial services competition is unique:

**Trust & Reputation Critical:** Financial services buyers prioritize trust, security, and reputation over features. Competitive positioning must address trust factors.

**Regulatory Complexity:** Compliance, certifications, regulatory positioning differentiate competitors. RegTech and compliance capabilities are competitive battlegrounds.

**Incumbent Advantage:** Established financial institutions have trust and relationships. Fintech disruptors must position against incumbent stability.

**Security & Compliance:** SOC 2, PCI DSS, SOX, financial data security - competitive requirements and differentiators.

**Product Complexity:** Financial products are complex. Feature comparison requires deep understanding of financial services.

**Customer Acquisition Cost:** High CAC in financial services means each competitive loss is expensive. Win rate improvements have oversized impact.

CIx provides competitive intelligence for financial services unique dynamics: trust positioning, regulatory intelligence, security comparisons, and market positioning.
