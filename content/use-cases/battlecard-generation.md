## How It Works (400 words)

#### Step 1: Select Competitor
Choose which competitor to create battlecard for. Or use AI competitor discovery.

#### Step 2: Choose Focus
Select battlecard focus:
- **Sales Focus:** Discovery questions, objection handling, win strategies
- **Marketing Focus:** Positioning, messaging, campaign intelligence
- **Product Focus:** Feature comparison, technical differentiation
- **Leadership Focus:** Strategic positioning, business model, market position

#### Step 3: AI Generates Battlecard
60 seconds later, comprehensive battlecard ready for review.

#### Step 4: Review & Refine
Review AI-generated content. Refine with AI modifications ("add more pricing detail") or manual edits.

#### Step 5: Publish & Distribute
Publish to CIx and automatically sync to Highspot, Seismic, Salesforce, or other platforms.

#### Step 6: Automatic Maintenance
CIx monitors competitor. When changes detected, battlecard refreshed automatically.

## Battlecard Structure (300 words)

#### What's Included in CIx Battlecards:

**Overview & Positioning:**
- Competitor description and positioning
- Target market and customers
- Key value propositions
- Market position

**Competitive Analysis:**
- Their strengths (honest assessment)
- Our advantages (differentiation)
- Key differentiators
- Competitive positioning

**Features & Capabilities:**
- Feature comparison
- Technical capabilities
- Product strengths and weaknesses
- UI/UX comparison

**Pricing Intelligence:**
- Pricing models and tiers
- Typical deal sizes
- Discount patterns
- ROI and value comparison

**Win Strategies:**
- How to position against them
- Discovery questions to ask
- Objection responses
- Talk tracks and messaging

**Source Attribution:**
- Every claim has source URL
- Last updated timestamp
- Verification status
