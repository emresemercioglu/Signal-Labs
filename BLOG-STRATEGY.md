## Blog ‚Üí Solutions Page Strategy

**All pages will be built in Version 1**, but blogs will complement and support them:

### How Blogs Support Solutions Pages:

1. **Write blog posts on related topics**
   - Each solution page can reference 2-3 relevant blog posts
   - Blog posts provide deeper dives on specific angles
   
2. **Link from solutions pages to relevant blog posts**
   - Internal linking strengthens SEO
   - Provides additional resources for engaged readers
   
3. **Use blog content to test messaging and angles**
   - Test different positioning approaches
   - Validate problem statements and pain points
   - Experiment with new angles before committing to page redesigns
   
4. **Repurpose best-performing blog content into page sections**
   - Successful blog sections can be adapted for solution pages
   - High-engagement content informs page structure
   
5. **Build backlinks to blog posts, which pass authority to solutions pages**
   - External links to blog posts boost domain authority
   - Internal links from blogs pass authority to solutions pages

## Blog Post Format/Template

### Standard Structure:
1. **Hook/Opening** (100-150 words)
   - Relatable problem or scenario
   - Set up the challenge
   
2. **Problem Deep Dive** (300-400 words)
   - Explain the problem in detail
   - Why traditional approaches fail
   - Real-world examples
   
3. **Solution/Framework** (500-700 words)
   - Introduce better approach
   - Step-by-step guidance
   - Best practices
   
4. **How CIx Helps** (300-400 words)
   - Connect to platform capabilities
   - Specific features that solve the problem
   - Not overly promotional
   
5. **Conclusion & CTA** (100-150 words)
   - Recap key takeaways
   - Clear next steps
   - CTA to demo or free trial

### Target Length:
- **1,500-2,000 words** for most posts
- **2,500-3,000 words** for comprehensive guides

### Voice & Tone:
- **Educational, not promotional**
- **Practical and actionable**
- **Industry insider perspective**
- **No jargon when avoidable**
