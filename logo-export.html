Recommended sizes and styles:

• Google (Organization Schema): 512x512 or 1024x1024 pixels - use Circle or Rounded

• Favicon: 512x512 pixels - use Circle for modern look

• LinkedIn profile/company: 400x400 or 1024x1024 pixels - use Square corners

• General use: 1024x1024 or higher for best quality

Tip: For Google search results, use Circle or Rounded at 512x512 minimum. For LinkedIn, use Square corners to avoid white gaps.
