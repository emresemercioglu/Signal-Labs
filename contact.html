Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Signal Labs

What happens during the free consultation?

We'll discuss your current competitive intelligence challenges, assess your competitive landscape, and provide initial recommendations. No sales pitch, just valuable insights.

How quickly can you get started?

For consulting engagements, we can typically start within 1-2 weeks. For the CIx platform, we're currently in beta and onboarding new users monthly.

Do you work with companies of all sizes?

Yes, we work with startups, scale-ups, and enterprise companies. Our consulting packages are flexible and the CIx platform is designed for teams of all sizes.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's exactly what the free consultation is for. We'll help you understand your options and recommend the best approach for your specific situation.

Is there a minimum engagement?

Our Strategic Assessment package is a 2-week engagement. For ongoing consulting, we work on quarterly contracts. The CIx platform has flexible monthly plans.

Do you offer training?

Yes, we provide training and workshops on competitive intelligence best practices, sales enablement, and how to use competitive insights effectively.
